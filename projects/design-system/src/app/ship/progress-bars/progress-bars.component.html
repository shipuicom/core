Progress Bar

Currently only have variants flat and raised more to come.

Variants

Progress bar variants can be set through the class, e.g. class="flat" . Available variants: base , flat , outlined , raised .

Colors

Progress bar colors can be set through the class, e.g. class="primary" . Available colors: primary , accent , warn , error , success .

Disabled

The progress bar can be set to disabled through the attribute, e.g. [disabled] .
