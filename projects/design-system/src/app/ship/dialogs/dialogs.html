Dialog

Utility component to create dialogs for modals, forms, and more.

Customization

The options attribute accepts a configuration object for: width , height , maxWidth , maxHeight , closeOnButton , closeOnEsc , and closeOnOutsideClick .

Variants

Dialogs support multiple layouts via the variant attribute. Valid options: default and type-b .

Slots

Structure your dialog content using the header , content , and footer attributes on container elements.
