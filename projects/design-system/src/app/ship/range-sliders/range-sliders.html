Range Slider

Binding

Two-way bindable value. Use [(ngModel)] or [formControl] .

Constraints

Set the range limits and increments using the min , max , and step attributes.

Visuals

Customize the look and feel:

  • color : primary , accent , warn , success , or error .
  • variant : base , thick , outlined , flat , or raised .
  • sharp : Use sharp corners instead of rounded.
  • always-show : Keep the value indicator visible at all times.

Unit

Specify a suffix for the value display using the unit attribute (e.g., unit="%" ).

Disabled

The slider can be disabled using the standard disabled attribute or [disabled] binding.
