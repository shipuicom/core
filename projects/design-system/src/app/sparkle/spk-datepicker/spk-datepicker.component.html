Datepicker

date

The selected date. Two-way bindable via [(date)] or [date] / (dateChange) for spk-datepicker and spk-datepicker-input .

endDate

The end date for range selection. Two-way bindable via [(endDate)] or [endDate] / (endDateChange) for spk-datepicker and spk-daterange-input .

asRange

Enable range selection mode. [asRange]="true" (default: false )

monthsToShow

Number of months to display at once. [monthsToShow]="2" (default: 1 )

disabled

Disable the datepicker. [disabled]="true"

masking

Date format mask for input display (e.g. 'mediumDate' ). Applies to spk-datepicker-input and spk-daterange-input .

closed

Event emitted when the picker closes. (closed) (returns selected date(s)).

startOfWeek

First day of the week (0 = Sunday, 1 = Monday, ...). [startOfWeek] (default: 1 ).

weekdayLabels

Custom weekday labels. [weekdayLabels]="['Su','Mo','Tu','We','Th','Fr','Sa']"
