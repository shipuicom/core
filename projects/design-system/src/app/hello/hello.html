Why ShipUI?

We were frustrated that most UI libraries look clunky and many teams fail to ship good-looking products. Most UI libs come with inconsistent API design, heavy dependencies, and monstrous CSS files.

ShipUI is our answer. Developed while building real-world applications like Duplicati , it focuses on what actually matters: agility, consistency, and style.
